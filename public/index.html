Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is BiteCheck's ingredient analysis?

BiteCheck uses advanced AI technology with over 98% accuracy in identifying ingredients and potential allergens from food labels.

Does BiteCheck work with all food products?

BiteCheck works with packaged foods that have an ingredient list or nutrition label. Our database includes over 100,000 products and grows daily.

Is my health data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect all your personal data and never share your information with third parties.
